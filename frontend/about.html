About The Shop

Sri Sai Liquor Shop is a dedicated premium liquor service provider committed to making exclusive and authentic beverages accessible to all. Founded and managed by Subba Reddy, Sri Sai operates as a local hub, meeting your celebration and collection needs with convenient, secure, and customer-oriented services.

Our mission is to bring top-tier Indian wines, beers, and whiskeys directly to people, ensuring every purchase is a mark of quality. This includes a curated selection of fine spirits carefully chosen for our discerning customers.

We also offer an easy-to-use takeaway ordering system, ensuring secure and convenient options for all customers.
